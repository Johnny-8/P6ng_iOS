Privacy Policy for P6ng

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Applies to Pong and P6ng

P6ng ("the app") is a network diagnostic utility that sends ICMP ping requests to hosts you specify, in order to measure network reachability and latency.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in is required to use the app.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the app.
  • No data leaves your device. The app does not communicate with any server operated by the developer or any third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device, using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Hostnames and IP addresses you enter or import for monitoring
  • Ping results (timestamps, response times, and success/failure status) for hosts you've pinged

This data is stored only in the app's local storage on your device. It is not backed up to iCloud, synced to other devices, or accessible to the developer. You can delete this data at any time using the in-app history-clearing controls, or by deleting the app.

Local Network Access

The app requests access to your local network in order to send ICMP ping requests to hosts on your network (such as routers, servers, or other devices) and to hosts you specify elsewhere on the internet. This access is used solely for the ping functionality you initiate within the app.

Sharing Your Data

If you choose to export ping history as a CSV file using the in-app share feature, that file is created locally on your device. Where it goes afterward (e.g., AirDrop, email, Files) is entirely your choice, made through Apple's standard share sheet. The developer has no visibility into or access to files you choose to export or share.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the support link provided on the App Store listing.
